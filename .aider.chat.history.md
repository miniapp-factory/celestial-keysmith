---

### **🔨 Core Gameplay Systems**

#### **1. Stardust Collection**

* Player travels to cosmic zones (Meteor Gardens, Nebula Mines, Comet Trails).
* Collects raw materials:

  * **Star Iron**
  * **Nebula Quartz**
  * **Void Glass**
  * **Aurora Threads**
* Resources have rarity and elemental affinities (solar, lunar, void, storm).

#### **2. Key Forging (Crafting System)**

* Craft keys using a multi-step forging mechanic:

  1. Smelt materials at the **Astral Forge**.
  2. Shape the key with celestial molds.
  3. Imbue magic through constellation patterns.
* Keys have stats:

  * **Alignment** (solar/lunar/void)
  * **Complexity** (basic / ornate / legendary)
  * **Attunement** (puzzle type unlocked)

#### **3. Celestial Vaults (Puzzle Rooms)**

Every vault is a self-contained challenge. Types include:

* **Constellation Logic Puzzles** – rotate star nodes to form required shapes.
* **Runic Lock Patterns** – align glyphs based on cryptic hints.
* **Astral Mechanisms** – move floating platforms, redirect starlight beams.
* **Temporal Gates** – time-based puzzles where cosmic currents shift.

Vault difficulties scale with the **complexity of the key** used.

#### **4. Player-Generated Clues**

* Each vault contains clues left by previous players:

  * Notes, sketches, constellation outlines, fragment messages, hints.
* System ensures:

  * Useful clues are surfaced to new players
  * Troll/low-quality clues are filtered via community rating
  * Clues have visual styles tied to the leaving player’s forge aesthetic

#### **5. Asynchronous Multiplayer Discovery**

* When players complete a vault:

  * They may leave behind a clue
  * They unlock someone else’s clue chain
* Vaults evolve based on community-driven puzzle paths.

---

### **🌀 Progression System**

#### **Keysmith Level**

* XP from:

  * Forging keys
  * Opening vaults
